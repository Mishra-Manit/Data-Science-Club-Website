Frequently Asked Questions

What would I do in Data Science Club?

IHS data science club will host multiple data science and AP statistics workshops along with competitions for each (also a combined data science + statistics competition). You will be exposed to various data science algorithms and libraries along with AP statistics review.

How can I sign up?

Fill out the form at the link: https://tinyurl.com/ds-interest

What is the AP Stats workshop?

We will go over all the AP statistics curriculum starting from mean, median, mode to chi-squared tests. This will help you get ready for the AP Stats test.

Which libaries will be covered?

We will go over data science tools such as Tableau, SK Learn, and more. By understanding these libraries, you will actually be able to draw insightful conclusions from large pieces of data.

Which algorithms will be covered?

We will go over how algorithms like Linear Regression, Naive-Bayes, Random Forest, and many more can be used to analyze data and draw patterns from it.
